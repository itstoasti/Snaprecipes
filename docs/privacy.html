1 Introduction

Welcome to Snap Recipes ("we," "our," "us," or the "Company"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App") and related services (collectively, the "Services").

By downloading, installing, or using Snap Recipes, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Services.

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of Snap Recipes, including users of our free tier and Pro subscribers. Certain data processing activities may only apply to Pro users (such as cloud sync features).

3 Information We Collect

We collect information in the following categories:

3.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and display name.
  • Recipe Data: Recipes you save, including titles, ingredients, instructions, images, source URLs, and any notes or modifications you make.
  • User Content: Photos you take or upload for recipe extraction, collections you create, and organizational preferences.
  • Communications: When you contact us for support, we collect the information you provide in your messages.

3.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, model, operating system version, unique device identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID), language settings, and time zone.
  • Usage Data: Features you use, actions you take within the App, frequency and duration of activities, and interaction patterns.
  • Performance Data: Crash reports, error logs, and diagnostic information to help us improve the App.
  • Network Information: IP address, internet service provider, and connection type (WiFi/cellular).

3.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Payment Information: When you subscribe to Pro, our payment processor (via Google Play) provides us with transaction confirmation, subscription status, and purchase history. We do not receive or store your credit card details.
  • Authentication Providers: If you sign in via Google or other third-party authentication, we receive your email address and basic profile information as authorized by you.

3.4 Sensitive Information

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information such as health data, biometric data, genetic data, religious beliefs, political opinions, or sexual orientation. Recipe data you save may incidentally contain dietary preference information (e.g., "vegan," "gluten-free"), which is stored as part of your recipe content.

5 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the Services: Process your recipes, sync data across devices, and deliver the core functionality of the App.
  • Process transactions: Manage your subscription, process payments through Google Play, and provide purchase confirmations.
  • Extract recipes: Process images and URLs you provide through our AI-powered extraction service to identify and structure recipe information.
  • Improve our Services: Analyze usage patterns, identify bugs, and develop new features based on user behavior.
  • Communicate with you: Send service-related notices, respond to support requests, and provide updates about the App.
  • Ensure security: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access to your account.
  • Comply with legal requirements: Meet our legal obligations and respond to lawful requests from authorities.

9 Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction:

Encryption

All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.3. Data at rest is encrypted using AES-256.

Access Controls

Strict access controls limit employee access to personal data on a need-to-know basis with audit logging.

Infrastructure Security

Our infrastructure providers maintain SOC 2 Type II compliance and undergo regular security audits.

Incident Response

We maintain incident response procedures and will notify affected users of any data breach as required by law.

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password and keep your device secure.
